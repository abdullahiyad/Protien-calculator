    Essential Health and Nutrition Tips

    Tips for Increasing Protein Intake:

    • Eat a variety of protein sources like meat, fish, and legumes.
    • Add protein to snacks such as yogurt and nuts.
    • Prepare protein-rich meals in advance.
    • Consider protein supplements if needed

    Tips for Drinking More Water:

    • Carry a water bottle with you throughout the day.
    • Use apps to remind you to drink water.
    • Add natural flavors to water if desired.
    • Eat fruits and vegetables high in water content.

    General Health Tips:

    • Maintain a balanced diet.
    • Exercise regularly.
    • Get enough sleep.
    • Reduce stress with relaxation techniques.
